Fast, Efficient, Interactive

MyWebSQL is the ultimate desktop replacement for managing your MySQL databases over the web. With interface that works just like your favourite desktop applications, you don't need to keep switching over webpages to get simple things done. Just login to your database and manage your database as if your are working on your desktop! Checkout online demo

Hassle Free Installation

Getting started is as simple as downloading the package and unpacking it on your choice of folder on the website. You only require a web server with php installed to get it up and running. No configuration required, no variables to be changed. Just open your favourite browser and hit the url to get going! See the complete list of features

Completely Open Source!

That's right. MyWebSQL is completely open source software and available free for download. You can add/edit/change whatever you like. It even supports themes so you can create your own to suit your needs! Download Now!
